About Us

The atmosphere at HueCafebar is unlike any other coffee house in Macon. We started our cafe business since 1970 and we evolved to cafe bar in 2018. We are a location where you can sit down and enjoy that specially brewed cup of coffee, not just a place to drop by for your morning brew. We have a place set aside for you to sit if you need to work. We have a comfortable seating area with a stone fireplace that is ideal for your tired mind and body if you wish to take a nap. We provide a delectable selection of Safehouse Coffee coffee brewed by our skilled baristas.
